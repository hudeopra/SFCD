The team returned to training and others also returned to training

August27,2020

The 1979/1980 season was the first time that CD San ​​Fernando competed in 2nd B, a category that only had two years of existence. It was made up of two quite powerful groups that included not only Andalusian, Ceutí, Extremadura or Castilian-Manchego teams, but also Catalans, Valencians and the Balearic Islands.

That summer 1954 fans became members of the club chaired by Ramírez Muñoz and which had its headquarters at Calle Manuel Roldán 19. The budget for the season was 26 million pesetas. Fewer partners than a priori thought.

For this first project, a prestigious technician like Martín Vences arrived, who had trained Rayo, Nástic and Logroñés, among others. There was a lot of movement in summer. The club managed to reinforce two positions with loaned players: the goal with the recreationalist Romero and the center of the field with Juan Antonio, who came from Cádiz. Already in the winter market, Paquito Pavón was transferred to Almería and left-handed Juan Abad was included in the negotiations. The spinal column was maintained
